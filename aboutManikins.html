What is a manikin-based simulation training?

Manikin-based simulations use manikins that mimic breathing, heart tones, and palpable pulses. These life-like manikins allow future and current physicians to practice true clinical skills in a safe and regulated environment.

Training for procedures such as spinal tap, also known as, lumbar puncture, are made easier with the aid of manikin-based simulations.

What are lumbar-puncture manikins?

Lumbar puncture and epidural simulators are designed for hands-on training and assessment in lumbar puncture and epidural procedures. Current lumbar puncture manikins have realistic tissue resistance and include a few interchangeable puncture blocks also consists of a fluid reservoir to simulate CSF.



The skills physicians gain include:

Palpation of lumbar spinous process

Needle positioning and insertion

Collection of cerebrospinal fluid (CSF)
